Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 31, 2026

This privacy policy describes how the OpenPulsechain Chrome Extension ("the Extension") handles your data. The Extension is a PulseChain analytics tool that provides token safety scores, wallet tracking, bridge statistics, and price data.

1. Data Stored Locally

All user data is stored locally on your device using chrome.storage. Nothing is synced to our servers. Locally stored data includes:

  • Wallet addresses — public blockchain addresses you enter
  • Wallet labels — names you assign to your wallets
  • Password hash — derived via PBKDF2-SHA256 with 100,000 iterations; your actual password is never stored
  • Notification preference — your on/off setting
  • Last activity timestamp — used for the auto-lock feature

2. External API Calls

The Extension makes network requests to the following services to provide its features:

  • safety.openpulsechain.com — token safety scores, alerts, bridge stats, wallet balances, smart money swaps
  • api.openpulsechain.com — token prices, search, price history
  • Database API (read-only) — recent tweets about PulseChain tokens
  • tokens.app.pulsex.com — token logo images

These requests transmit only the data strictly necessary to fulfill the request (e.g., a token contract address or a public wallet address). No personal information is included in these calls.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

The Extension does not:

  • Collect personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Track or access your browsing history
  • Transmit wallet addresses to any third party outside of openpulsechain.com APIs
  • Store or access private keys or seed phrases
  • Require account creation or sign-up
  • Use cookies or tracking pixels
  • Sell, share, or monetize any user data

4. Data Security

Your lock password is protected using PBKDF2-SHA256 with a random 16-byte salt and 100,000 iterations. The Extension includes brute-force protection and automatic locking after a period of inactivity. Since all data resides locally on your device, there is no server-side database to breach.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

All data persists locally until you explicitly remove it. You can delete all stored data at any time by uninstalling the Extension or clearing its storage through your browser's extension management page.

6. Third-Party Services

The Extension relies on the APIs listed in Section 2. These services are operated by OpenPulsechain and PulseX. No data is shared with advertising networks, analytics providers, or any other third parties.

7. Children's Privacy

The Extension is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at contact@openpulsechain.com.
