## Detailed description

OpenPulsechain is the essential security companion for PulseChain users. Get real-time safety scores, scam detection, and portfolio tracking — all from your browser toolbar.

**Token Safety**
Instantly check any PulseChain token's safety score (0-100) before you trade. Our scoring analyzes contract security, liquidity depth, holder distribution, deployer reputation, and token age. Honeypot detection included.

**Scam Detection**
Automatic detection of fake token clones impersonating popular tokens like HEX, PLS, PLSX, and INC. Visual warnings on your portfolio if a scam clone is detected.

**Transaction Guard**
When you interact with PulseChain DEXs (PulseX, 9mm, Piteas), OpenPulsechain checks the token safety BEFORE you sign. Get warned about dangerous tokens directly on the swap page.

**Portfolio Tracker**
Track your PulseChain wallet with live prices, 30-day performance sparkline, token allocation breakdown, and value history charts. Prices update dynamically as you explore the historical chart.

**Live Market Data**
- Real-time prices for PLS, HEX, PLSX, INC, and PRVX
- 24h price changes with trend indicators
- Bridge monitor (deposit/withdrawal volumes)
- Smart money movements (whale swaps)
- Recent security alerts

**Holder Leagues**
See your rank among all holders for core PulseChain tokens. From Turtle to Poseidon — discover where you stand.

**Security First**
- Password-protected with PBKDF2-SHA256 encryption (100K iterations)
- Auto-lock after inactivity
- All data stored locally — nothing leaves your browser
- No account required, no personal data collected
- Open source: https://github.com/openpulsechain/openpulsechain

**Supported DEXs**
PulseX, 9mm.pro, Piteas.io, DexScreener

Built by the community, for the community.
